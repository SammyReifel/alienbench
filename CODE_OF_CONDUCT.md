# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep project spaces respectful, focused, and welcoming to people with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about technical disagreements.
- Keep discussions on the project, research method, code, and documentation.
- Respect privacy and do not post credentials, private logs, or sensitive transcripts.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Posting secrets, credentials, or exploit details in public channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct. Serious security issues should follow `SECURITY.md`.
